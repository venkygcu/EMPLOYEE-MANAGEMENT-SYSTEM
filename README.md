## 🎯 Overview

The Employee Management System is a complete solution for managing employee data, workflows, and organizational processes. It provides HR departments and management teams with tools to handle recruitment, employee records, performance management, and payroll administration from a centralized platform.

## ✨ Features

### Core Functionality
- **Employee Management**
  - Create, update, and maintain employee records
  - Store personal and professional information
  - Track employment history and status

- **Recruitment Management**
  - Job posting and applicant tracking
  - Resume management
  - Interview scheduling and feedback

- **Onboarding Process**
  - Automated onboarding workflows
  - Task assignment and tracking
  - Document management

- **Performance Management**
  - Performance review tracking
  - Goals and objectives management
  - Employee ratings and feedback

- **Leave Management**
  - Leave request submission
  - Leave approval workflow
  - Leave balance tracking

- **Payroll Management**
  - Salary structure management
  - Payroll processing
  - Tax and deduction calculations

- **Administrative Functions**
  - User and role management
  - Department management
  - Reporting and analytics
  - System settings and configurations

- **User Authentication**
  - Secure login system
  - Role-based access control (RBAC)
  - User permissions management

## 🚀 Usage

### Getting Started
1. Log in with your credentials
2. Navigate to the appropriate module based on your role
3. Follow the on-screen instructions for specific tasks

### Key Workflows

#### Adding a New Employee
1. Go to Employee Management
2. Click "Add Employee"
3. Fill in the required information
4. Submit the form

#### Processing Leave Request
1. Navigate to Leave Management
2. Review pending requests
3. Approve or reject requests
4. System updates leave balance automatically

#### Creating a Job Posting
1. Access Recruitment Module
2. Click "Create Job Posting"
3. Enter job details and requirements
4. Publish the posting
